"Shape" Shifter

Kyr has an incredible ability to change his identity, form, and aspects by entering the "mind" of departed spirits trapped in the "Trauma" level of the AfterLife. Whenever a spirit needs help, a "Trauma" door appears randomly, giving Kyr a chance to offer assistance. To demonstrate this, I've created a collection of simple morphing shapes with basic colors on a canvas. In the black area, a solid orange square represents Kyr's typical state. In the white area, a constantly changing circle represents the endless possibilities that exist when crossing a door or being in the AfterLife. If you move the circle quickly, you'll see an illusion of many circles, each with a different color, representing the mixed realities within the AfterLife.
